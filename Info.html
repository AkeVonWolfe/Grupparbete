ACTIMOVR HERO12 Black Features

The ACTIMOVR HERO12 Black is the ultimate action camera designed for adventurers and creators. It offers 5.3K video at 60fps, 27MP photos, and HyperSmooth 5.0 stabilization. Built to be durable and waterproof up to 33 feet, it's perfect for extreme conditions.

  • Resolution: 5.3K video, 27MP photos
  • Waterproof: Up to 33ft (10m)
  • Battery Life: Up to 2 hours continuous recording
  • Special Features: HyperSmooth 5.0, TimeWarp 3.0, Voice Control
  • Price: $299.99
